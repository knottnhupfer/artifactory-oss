Release Bundles

Upgrade to JFrog Enterprise+ and distribute your software securely, reliably and automatically.

Learn More

  • Release Bundles are distributed with JFrog Distribution to Artifactory Edge Nodes and are part of the Enterprise+ platform.

  • Release Bundles group together the contents that are part of your release, providing the bill of materials for your software releases that can then be pushed to your edge devices.

  • Release Bundles are secure and immutable, ensuring that no manipulation can be made by unauthorized users while distributing them.
